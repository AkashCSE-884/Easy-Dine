Our Story

Diners today expect a smooth experience from the moment they make their reservation. As a result, online reservation systems are in fierce competition. However, apart from(opentable.ca), there is still no platform or website in existence in Halifax that provides a streamlined process for booking a restaurant table for the consumer and ensures guests a space there at restaurants of their preference once they visit. Today, ever more people have continuous internet access.

A solution to this problem would a table booking web application – EasyTable.

  • This application will allow customers full flexibility to make a reservation without having to connect with anyone.
  • Guaranteeing that diners will obtain their table at the time and location they have specified.
  • Provide restaurants with the opportunity to handle their spaces more effectively, increase customer loyalty, enhance profitability, and the eating experience.
